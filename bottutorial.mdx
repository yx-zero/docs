## Purchase Related Commands

**/purchase** to buy products, enter the amount you want to buy in **quantity** field

## Credit Managing Commands

**/redeem** to use a redeem code, enter the code in **code** field

**/balance** to view your balance

**/transfer** to transfer your credit to another user, select a user in the **user** section and enter the amount you want to send in the **credit** field

## Other Commands

**/stock** to view our stock

**/top** to view your credit ranking(we got a rank based on user's credit balance)
