## Purchase Related Commands

**/purchase** to buy products, enter the amount you want to buy in **quantity** field

**/replace** to replace an order(refund you) if the product you bought is invalid, enter the orderid you want to replace in the **orderid** field

## Credit Managing Commands

**/deposit** to deposit credits using LTC, enter the amount you want to deposit in **credits** field. minimum 100 credits

**/redeem** to use a redeem code, enter the code in **code** field

**/balance** to view your balance

## Other Commands

**/stock** to view our stock
